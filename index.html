Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

What exactly is a "qualified lead"?

A qualified lead is a prospective patient within your service area who has expressed interest in a specific dental service you offer, provided valid contact information, and is not a current patient of your practice.

How does the lead guarantee work?

If we don't deliver the minimum number of qualified leads specified in your package in any given month, you'll receive a credit toward the following month's service fee (25% for Starter, 30% for Growth, 35% for Premium).

Do I need to sign a long-term contract?

Our standard agreement is for 12 months, which allows us to optimize your campaigns for maximum performance. We also offer month-to-month options at a slightly higher rate.

How quickly will I start seeing results?

Most clients begin receiving leads within the first week of campaign launch. It typically takes 30-60 days to fully optimize campaigns for maximum performance.

Can I choose which dental services to promote?

Absolutely. During onboarding, we'll work with you to identify which services to focus on based on your practice goals, profitability, and market opportunity.

What makes DentaLeads different from other dental marketing agencies?

Three key differences: 1) We guarantee a minimum number of leads, 2) We exclusively focus on dental practices, and 3) We offer transparent, all-inclusive pricing with no hidden fees.
